Fan Favorite Meals

Studio Ghibli films are filled with food scenes so mouthwatering and comforting that they've inspired fans across the world to recreate them at home. These memorable dishes go beyond aestheticsâ€”they often represent connection, comfort, and the beauty found in ordinary moments. From cozy breakfasts to enchanting feasts, Ghibli-inspired meals have gained a strong following online, with fans enthusiastically sharing their interpretations and recipes.

  • Spirited Away: Feast

    One of the most iconic food scenes is the giant feast that Chihiro's parents devour at the beginning of Spirited Away. The meats, dumplings, and overflowing plates, this scene left a lasting impression on the audience. While the exact dishes are inspired by various cuisines, fans have attempted to replicate the experience by creating multi-course spreads with bao buns, pork, and other flavorful items.

  • Howl's Moving Castle: Bacon & Eggs

    In Howl's Moving Castle, this simple breakfast becomes a cozy, heartfelt scene shared among the characters. Although the thick cut bacon has proven challenging to find, fans still love attempting to recreate this dish. Many even try to mimic the rustic cooking style shown in the film, using cast-iron pans and minimal ingredients.

  • Ponyo: Ramon

    Sosuke and Ponyo enjoy a simple yet delicious bowl of instant ramen with green onions, ham, and egg. Though it's an everyday meal, fans still adore the dish.
